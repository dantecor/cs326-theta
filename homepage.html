Are you looking to find a great place to eat but find your options limited by your allergies/intolerances? Good news, Food Match is the website for you! Food Match allows you to specify any kind of allergies/food intolerances you may have and helps you find restaurants in your area that cater to those needs. Now that Food Match has you covered, you want to go through the trouble of feeling like you can't find a place to eat.

From milk allergies to shellfish allergies, Food Match helps you find restaurants with menu items that can be safely consumed regardless of what you are allergic to. Currently, the site can help you find delicious meals even if you have any of the top eight most common food allergies: Wheat, fish, milk, eggs, peanuts, soybeans, tree nuts, and crustacean shellfish. Simply specify which allergens you are allergic to when you create your account, and we will find you a list of restaurant menus that are friendly for anyone with such allergies.

Food Match currently covers restaurants across Amherst, Massachusetts. Each of these restaurants feature menus with items that can be safely consumed by people with the top eight most common food allergies. Restaurant owners can submit their restaurant information for a chance to be featured in Food Match. Simply create an account and click on the add Restaurant button of the main screen to be featured on the site.
